About Us

We are a passionate team of developers, designers, and spreadsheet enthusiasts who believe that everyone should have access to powerful, easy-to-use tools to work with data.

At Celllang, our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to effortlessly harness the power of data and create meaningful insights without the need for extensive programming or analytical expertise. In today's data-driven world, access to valuable information is no longer limited to large corporations. Small businesses and individuals now have the ability to leverage data to make informed decisions. However, the challenge lies in deriving insights and collaborating effectively with numbers.


Traditional data programming tools are undeniably powerful, but they often come with a high barrier to entry. These tools require proficiency in complex programming languages like Python and SQL, making them inaccessible to many. On the other hand, spreadsheets are more approachable but come with inherent limitations. They are prone to errors, difficult to scale, and not designed for seamless collaboration.


This is why we developed Celllang. We firmly believe that everyone should have the ability to utilize, model, and comprehend data in meaningful ways, regardless of their skill set or perspective. With Celllang, you can generate and modify spreadsheets effortlessly by writing equations in a familiar code-like syntax. Our language automates the generation of spreadsheet formulas, saving you from the complexities of traditional spreadsheet formula syntax.


We are bridging the gap between tools for programmers and tools for non-programmers, enabling more individuals to actively participate in our data-rich world. Celllang empowers you to work with data intuitively, unlocking your potential to make informed decisions and collaborate effectively. Join us on this exciting journey as we revolutionize the way people interact with data. Experience the power of Celllang and discover a new level of ease and productivity in generating and manipulating spreadsheets.
